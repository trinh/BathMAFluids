Exercise 3. Derivation of the Cauchy equation.

In this problem, you will derive the Cauchy equation (7.5.2) for the conservation of linear momentum in a more general fluid (the Navier–Stokes equation only applies to Newtonian fluids). We will consider a small cube of fluid as shown in Figure 7.5.1, and apply the Reynolds Transport Theorem (3.1.2).
