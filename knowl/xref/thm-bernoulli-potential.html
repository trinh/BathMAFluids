Theorem 3.5.8. Bernoulli’s equation for steady irrotational flow.

For an incompressible and irrotational flow, Bernoulli’s theorem states that
\begin{equation} B(\bx) \equiv \frac{p}{\rho} + \frac{1}{2} |\bu|^2 + \chi = \textrm{constant everywhere},\tag{3.5.10} \end{equation}
where \(\bg = -\nabla \chi\) is the body force.
